Alkyl radical scavenging test

Reagents

     AAPH 40mM
     AAPH 40mM
     All solution is prepared in PBS pH 7.4

Procedure

  1. 20μl DW
  2. 20μl sample
  3. 20μl AAPH (40mM)
  4. 20μl POBN
  5. Mixtures are incubate for 30 min at 37°C
  6. After 30min transfer into capillary tube
  7. Record the spectrum by ESR spectrophotometer.

ESR Condition

     Power: 10mW
     Amplitude: 1x1000
     Modulation width: 0.2mT
     Sweep width: 10mT
     Sweep time: 30sec
40mM POBN [α-(4-Pyridyl-1-oxcide)-N-tert_butylnitrone]MW 194.24g/mol 0.1g/12.8ml
40mM AAPH [2,2’-Azobis(2-methylpropionamidine) dihydrochloride] 0.5424g/50ml
