1. Fucoidan separation protocol
  2. Freeze-dried, salt-removed powdered sample (250g)
  3. Depigmentation with 95% EtOH
  4. Suspending in EtOH containing 10% formaldehyde & wash with EtOH to remove formaldehyde and dry it
  5. Suspend each 100 g of algae powder in 1L of DW and adjust pH to 4.5
  6. 4.5로 통일한다. 시간이 허락한다면 pH가 4.5에서 안정화 될때까지 여러번 반복한다.
  7. Celluclast assisted extraction using 0.5% enzyme at 50℃ for 24h (add more celluclast for a better yield)
  8. Heat inactivation of the enzyme (keep in boiling water till the inside temperature stay > 90℃ for at least 10 min)
  9. Adjust pH to 8.0 followed by alcalase assisted hydrolysis of proteins at 50 ℃ for > 3h
  10. Heat inactivation of the enzyme
  11. Adjust pH to 5.0 with HCl followed by addition of CaCl2 solution
  12. Clarification of the debris by centrifugation
  13. Adjust pH to 7.0 and concentrated the solution to 1/3 of its original volume
  14. Addition of 3 volumes of 95% EtOH to initiate the precipitation of the polysaccharides (keep in cool room for > 8h)
  15. Separate polysaccharides by centrifugation
  16. Washing polysaccharides using EtOH
  17. Dissolve polysaccharides in DW and lyophilization
  18. Separation (size exclusion and/or ion exchange (NaCl, 0 to 1.3 M gradient)
